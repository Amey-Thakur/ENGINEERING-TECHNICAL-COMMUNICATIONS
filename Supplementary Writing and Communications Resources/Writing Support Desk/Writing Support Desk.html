The University of Windsor Student Success and Leadership Centre operates the Writing Support Desk, a free service located on the main floor of the Leddy Library. Writing Support Desk appointments are one-on-one meetings with training writing advisors who will work with you to improve your academic writing. The Writing Support Desk also holds occasional workshops. Note that this is not a simple proofreading service.

If you feel your writing has room for improvement -- and it almost always will -- I wholeheartedly recommend it. My experience teaching this course tells me that students who take the time to schedule an appointment with this service virtually always improve their performance.
