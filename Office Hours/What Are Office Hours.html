What Are Office Hours?

Every Tuesday and Thursday during the semester, excluding Reading Week and any other announced absences, I (Prof. Jesse Ziter) will be available for virtual consultation to all Engineering Technical Communications Sections 1, 4, and 7 students from 8:30 to 11:00 a.m. 

Do I Need to Attend Office Hours?

Office Hours are optional. They operate on a drop-in basis: If you choose to attend, you do not need to make an appointment beforehand. 

Where Is Your Office?

For Winter 2023, Office Hours will take place in Microsoft Teams. I am happy to meet with students using videoconferencing, audio chat, or text chat (whichever you prefer!).
